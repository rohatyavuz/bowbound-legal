Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Short version. Bowbound does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your game progress stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Bowbound is an offline game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or phone number.
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, advertising ID).
  • Location data.
  • Contacts, photos, or files.
  • Usage analytics or telemetry.
  • Crash logs or diagnostic data sent to us.

2. Information Stored Locally

Your game progress (level completion, stars, gold, gems, achievements, settings) is stored only on your device. This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app permanently removes this data.

3. In-App Purchases

Bowbound offers optional in-app purchases (gem packs and the Starter Pack) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not see, collect, or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs your payment data. Purchases are validated through Apple's StoreKit framework and a stateless receipt verification endpoint that processes only the Apple-provided receipt token and discards it after verification.

4. Third-Party Services

Bowbound does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, social, or tracking SDKs. The only external service we communicate with is Apple's App Store for purchase validation.

5. Children's Privacy

Bowbound is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect personal information from anyone, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

Because no personal data leaves your device, there is nothing to request, export, or delete from us. You have full control of your data through iOS Settings (manage in-app purchases via your Apple ID, delete the app to remove all local data).

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how Bowbound handles data, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be announced in-game.

8. Contact

Questions? Email bowbound.support@gmail.com.
